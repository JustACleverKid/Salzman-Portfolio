    About Me

  • My name is Jack Salzman and I am 13.

    Things that I learned

  • One of my favorite things that I've learned in this course is how to use webforms. They are a fun and simple way to record data, and I really understand them.
  • One of my least favorite things in this course are flexboxes. The way that they work makes absolutely zero sense, and half the time even if you enter in all of the right data, something still goes wrong.
  • One of the best lessons that this course has taught me has got to be time management, and that I should work to get my assignments done as early as possible.
